About Diwali

Diwali, the festival of lights, is celebrated with great enthusiasm and joy across India and by Hindus worldwide. It symbolizes the victory of light over darkness, good over evil, and knowledge over ignorance. Families light lamps, burst crackers, share sweets, and exchange gifts.

It's a time for family gatherings, prayers, and new beginnings. We wish you and your loved ones a very Happy and Prosperous Diwali!
